WHO

WE ARE

Lomomanila is not just a group, but a community. Loose as it maybe, but a common denominator exist.

A non-profit and non-commercial association of film photographers with the desire to explore the creative possibilities of analog film.

WHAT

WE DO

We are all different but we are here to appreciate everyone, to collaborate for the time being and see how we can have fun with our cameras.

WHY

WE DO IT

We believe photography should be shared, and people all have their way of shooting.

It is Lomomanila's culture and more than anything else, collaboration.
